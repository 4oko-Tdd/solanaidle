What Is It

A week-long roguelike where the weekend boss is the whole point.

Roguelike Loop

Every Monday, start fresh. Build a unique character through missions, upgrades, and random perk choices. No two epochs play the same.

Community Boss

Every weekend, Protocol Leviathan spawns. The whole community must take it down together — or nobody gets drops. Real stakes.

Real Stakes

Fail a mission — lose a life. Time can't be skipped or purchased. No cheats. No pay-to-win. Time is the only resource.

Why Seeker

Built for early adopters.

Seeker owners are crypto-native early adopters. Early adopters want three things: status, competition, and shared rituals. Seeker Node delivers all three — every week.

01

Status

Rare boss drops persist forever. Leviathan Scale, NFT Artifacts — visible in your wallet. The longer you play, the stronger your base. Early players earn advantages that can't be bought.

03

08 / 11
Ecosystem Integration

Four integrations. Each serves a core mechanic.

Solana Mobile

Identity · Ownership · Distribution

One tap to sign in — no seed phrase, no friction. Your wallet is your identity, your character, and your loot vault. Native to Seeker, deployed on the dApp Store.

MWA v2 · wallet as loot vault · native discovery

MagicBlock

Real-time HP · Zero-fee Progress · VRF

Three uses: VRF for provably-fair epoch bonuses, ER for zero-fee player progress tracking, ER for live boss HP broadcast to all clients via WebSocket.

3 Anchor programs deployed on devnet

Metaplex Core

NFT Relics · Achievement Badges

Boss drops minted as Core assets with Attributes plugin — direct to your Seeker wallet. Server mints with zero player signatures. Rare loot lives on-chain, yours forever.

Seeker Node: Relics · Achievements

Jupiter

Intel Tab · Daily & Weekly Quests

In-game Intel tab with daily quests (Price Scout, Token Scan, PnL Report) and weekly quests (Micro Swap). Rewards: resources + temporary boosts.

Bridges idle gameplay to Solana DeFi
