# 1. Core Free Model

Every Epoch:
- 1 free Boss Entry
- 3 Lives
- Standard contribution scaling

# 2. SKR Utility Layer (Controlled & Capped)

## 2.1 Reconnect Protocol (Main SKR Sink)

### Mechanic

During the World Boss:
- Player has a % chance to become "Node Destabilized"
- This temporarily removes them from contributing damage

Player may activate:

### Reconnect Protocol
Cost: X SKR
Limit: 1 per epoch

Effect:
- Rejoin the Hunt
- Restore passive damage contribution
- No additional bonus multiplier

Purpose:
- Agency recovery, not power boost
- Emotional save moment
- Creates SKR sink without whale abuse

Hard Cap:
- Maximum 1 paid reconnect per epoch

---

## 2.2 Overload Amplifier (Strategic Option)

Optional SKR usage before triggering OVERLOAD:

Cost: Y SKR
Limit: 1 per epoch

Effect:
- +10% to final OVERLOAD burst
- Does NOT stack
- Does NOT multiply contribution beyond defined cap

Purpose:
- Tactical decision
- Resource + SKR tradeoff
- Minor edge, not dominance

---

## 2.3 Raid License (Optional Upgrade)

Free Entry:
- Normal contribution scaling

Premium Raid License (per epoch):
Cost: Z SKR

Grants:
- +5% contribution efficiency
- Cosmetic boss aura
- Priority damage animation effect
- Exclusive leaderboard badge for that week

Does NOT:
- Increase drop odds directly
- Increase roll count
- Stack across purchases

Limit: 1 per epoch

Purpose:
- Soft subscription model
- Status signaling
- SKR alignment without economy distortion

# 3. SKR Distribution Model

Every SKR spent is automatically distributed:

- 50% Burn
- 30% Seeker Treasury
- 20% Seasonal Reward Reserve

No pooled gambling system.
No direct redistribution of player funds.

---

# 4. Drop System Integrity

Boss Drops remain based on:
- Contribution %
- RNG (server-side / VRF-based when applicable)

SKR purchases do NOT:
- Add extra roll attempts
- Add extra drop slots
- Guarantee rare loot
- Multiply drop chances

Fairness remains intact.

---

# 5. Anti-Whale Safeguards

- All SKR mechanics capped per epoch
- No stacking purchases
- No unlimited reentries
- Contribution scaling capped
- No purchasable extra lives

---
